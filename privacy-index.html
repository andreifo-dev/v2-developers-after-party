Privacy Policy



At Developers After Party, we value the privacy of our clients and we are committed to protecting their personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard the information provided by our clients. By using our website and submitting your personal information, you consent to the practices described in this policy.


1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information directly from our clients through our website or other communication channels. The types of personal information we may collect include:

1.1. Full Name: We collect your full name to address you properly and provide personalized communication.

1.2. Company Name: If provided, we collect your company name to understand your business requirements and tailor our services accordingly.

1.3. Telephone Number: We collect your telephone number to contact you regarding your project, address any inquiries, or provide updates.

1.4. E-mail Address: We collect your e-mail address to communicate with you, send project-related information, respond to inquiries, and provide updates on our services.

1.5. Project Details/Information: We collect project-specific details and information to better understand your requirements and deliver personalized solutions. This may include project descriptions, specifications, goals, and any other relevant information you choose to provide.


2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1. Communication: We use your personal information, such as your full name, telephone number, and e-mail address, to contact you regarding your project, respond to inquiries, provide updates, and discuss relevant details.

2.2. Advertising Campaigns: With your consent, we may use your personal information to inform you about our services, promotions, special offers, and other relevant information. We may use your e-mail address for this purpose, and you can opt out of such communications at any time.

2.3. Service Improvement: We may use the project details and information you provide to improve our services, develop new features, enhance user experience, and ensure customer satisfaction.


3. Data Sharing and Protection

3.1. Third-Party Disclosure: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent. Your information is strictly used within Developers After Party for the purposes mentioned above.

3.2. Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party service providers to assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or providing services to you. These service providers have access to your personal information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

3.3. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as subpoenas or court orders.

3.4. Data Security: We implement reasonable security measures to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


4. Your Choices and Rights

4.1. Updating Information: You may update or correct your personal information by contacting us through the provided contact details.

4.2. Opting Out: You can opt out of receiving promotional e-mails from us by following the instructions provided in the e-mail or contacting us directly. However, please note that even if you opt out of receiving promotional communications, we may still send you non-promotional messages related to your projects or our ongoing business relationship.


5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete that information from our systems.

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.
